Privacy Policy – Triple T Cab

Effective Date: 27/05/2025

At Triple T Cab, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you provide through our booking form.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following details when you submit the booking form:

  • Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Pickup Location
  • Drop Location
  • Date and Time of Travel

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for processing your cab booking. When you submit the form:

  • Your booking details are sent to our team via WhatsApp.
  • We use this information to contact you and assign an available cab.

3. Data Sharing

- We share your booking information with trusted cab owners and drivers only for the purpose of fulfilling your ride request.
- The shared details include your name, mobile number, pickup and drop locations, and travel date/time.
- We do not share your information with any other third parties such as advertisers, marketers, or data analytics companies.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal data. However, since information is transmitted via WhatsApp, please avoid submitting any sensitive personal or financial information in the form.

5. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request deletion of your submitted booking information
  • Ask questions about our data usage policies
